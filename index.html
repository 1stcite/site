The Conference in Full

With 1stCite, attendees can view and discuss every talk, presenters can hear every question and comment, and you can observe and measure it all in real time.

Engaging Science

Conferences are rare gatherings of expert communities. What happens during a presentation is everything that peer review aspires to be — real-time expert scrutiny, cross-pollination of ideas, and the collective reaction of that scientific community to new findings. Conferences create tremendous value. 1stCite helps more fully capture that value.

The Untapped Value
Every conference creates more value than it captures.
Conferences are remarkable gatherings of the world's experts compressing vast quantities of intellectual energy into a single time and place. Time constraints and practical realities make capturing that potential energy daunting. By extending the conference presentation in time and space, 1stCite redefines what's possible.

Parallel sessions

The structure of a typical conference means attendees miss most presentations, content they traveled across the world to see.

Time constraints

Presentations routinely end with unanswered questions. Every one that goes unasked is a missed opportunity.

Groundbreaking science disappears

After the conference, groundbreaking science disappears in the long wait to publication.

An untapped social network

Scientific societies are natural, powerful social networks focused on research — yet most of that network's energy is compressed into a few days a year. By creating a model for sharing presentations easily online, the conference and the conversations become unstuck in time.

Beyond the Annual Meeting
With 1stCite, the conference can spread throughout the year.
Your society is a year-round research community. 1stCite gives it the infrastructure to act like one — with presentations, discussion, and expert engagement happening continuously, not just during a few days in a convention center.
The Solution
Every presentation gets a structured home for discussion.
Before, during, and after the meeting — structured conversation between presenters, attendees, and the broader community.
For Attendees

See more. Ask more. Miss less.

  • Scan presentations to decide which ones to attend and prepare and even ask questions in advance
  • Ask questions and comment on talks you can't attend in person
  • Continue the discussion with presenters and other attendees after the session
  • Search talks and get recommendations about which sessions to see
  • Share and promote talks on social media
For Presenters

More feedback. Broader reach.

  • Get substantive feedback — including questions before and after you present
  • Reach attendees who couldn't make it to your session
  • 1stCite is working with SAI, a nonprofit dedicated to scientific integrity, to provide DOIs and create a permanent open repository for conference science
For Organizers & Societies

Data. Reach. Year-round value.

  • See which talks attendees visit, comment on, star, and share
  • Extend the value and reach of the conference far beyond the event
  • Give members a tool to discuss research throughout the year
  • Add presentations on a rolling basis — not just at the annual meeting
